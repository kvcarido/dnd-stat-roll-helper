Stat Roll Helper

This side project serves as a personal quest for learning in public, a fun and productive way to apply my knowledge of front-end technologies into a tool that can be useful for DnD enthusiasts who can't be bothered with the repetitious act of rolling for stats. Unless you're into that process, then don't let me stop you!

There are many ways to calculate your character's abilities, but for the sake of simplicity I'll be following this method:

  • Roll a 6-sided die, four times
  • The lowest roll will be dropped
  • The sum of the highest three rolls will be the value for that attribute
